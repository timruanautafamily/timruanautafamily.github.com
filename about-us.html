a few words about us

HYDROTECH was established in 1995 primarily to supply the mining and mineral extraction industry. From those early days Apex Hydraulics has grown to be one of the most respected companies in the hydraulics industry.

The original purpose of the company was simply to offer a repair service, but due to demand for custom built equipment, this provided an ideal platform for HYDROTECH to diversify into the manufacturing and sales sector of the industry.

providing quality repair services

Choosing the right hydraulic equipment is key to ensuring that your entire hydraulic system works efficiently and smoothly.

A quality hydraulic hose will work to transfer hydraulic fluids (such as oil) effectively from one part of your machine to another. A burst in the hose could result in leakage, which can make your entire hydraulic system completely inefficient. Not only could this become dangerous, but your equipment could lose hydraulic power – leaving you with little choice but to invest in costly and time consuming repairs.

If you find yourself in need of hydraulic hose repair or hydraulic cylinder repair, it’s never a good idea to wait too long before you find a hydraulic repair company.
